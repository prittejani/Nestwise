1. Who We Are

Swarup is an Indian grooming and lifestyle app for iOS, built to help men and women discover personalized grooming routines, style tips, and self-care guidance in Hinglish.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights around it. By using Swarup, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

Swarup collects only the information you voluntarily provide during onboarding to personalise your experience. No account or login is required.

Onboarding preferences (stored locally on your device):

  • Gender (Man / Woman)
  • Age group (e.g. 18–25, 26–35)
  • Skin tone (Fair / Wheatish / Dusky / Deep)
  • Face shape (Oval / Round / Square / Heart / Oblong)
  • Skin type (Oily / Dry / Combination / Normal / Sensitive)
  • Hair type (Straight / Wavy / Curly / Thick / Fine)

App usage data (stored locally on your device):

  • Daily grooming routine completion status
  • Streak count and last active date
  • Tips saved / bookmarked by you
  • In-app purchase status (Swarup Pro)
✓ No email required ✓ No phone number ✓ No real name ✓ No location data ✓ No camera access ✓ No contacts access

3. Where Your Data Lives

All personal preferences and usage data are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework (local on-device storage). This data is:

  • Never uploaded to any server owned by Swarup
  • Automatically included in your device's iCloud backup if you have iCloud enabled (this is an Apple feature — Swarup does not control it)
  • Deleted permanently when you delete the app

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Swarup displays ads powered by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant ads. This data is collected and processed by Google, not by Swarup.

What AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only with your consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • IP address (approximate location for ad targeting)
  • App interaction data (ad views, clicks)
  • Device type and OS version

Important notes:

  • We never share your grooming profile data (skin type, hair type, etc.) with Google AdMob
  • Ads are served based on general contextual signals, not your personal grooming data
  • You can opt out of personalised ads in iOS Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising, or by upgrading to Swarup Pro (completely ad-free)

Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

💳
5. In-App Purchases (Swarup Pro)

Swarup Pro subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store using StoreKit. Swarup does not collect or store any payment information.

  • All billing is handled by Apple — we never see your card details
  • We only receive a confirmation token from Apple confirming your purchase status
  • To manage or cancel your subscription: iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

Apple's App Store privacy practices: apple.com/legal/privacy

6. How We Use Your Data

We use your on-device data for one purpose only — to personalise your Swarup experience:

  • Show grooming tips relevant to your skin type, hair type, and gender
  • Build and display your personalised daily routine
  • Track your grooming streak and completed routines
  • Remember your saved tips across app sessions
  • Determine which features are available (free vs Pro)

We do not use your data for profiling, behavioural tracking, selling to third parties, or any purpose other than making the app work better for you.

8. Children's Privacy

Swarup is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. However, certain content (makeup tips, skin care advice) is most relevant to users aged 13 and above.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no account or login is required and all data stays on-device, there is minimal privacy risk for younger users.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at privacy@swarup.app and we will address it promptly.

9. Your Rights & Choices

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete all data: Uninstall Swarup from your device. All locally stored preferences, routines, and history are permanently deleted.
  • Reset preferences: Go to Profile → Preferences → Edit and change any of your grooming profile settings at any time.
  • Opt out of personalised ads: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable tracking for Swarup. Or upgrade to Swarup Pro for a completely ad-free experience.
  • Manage subscription: iOS Settings → Your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Swarup Pro.
  • Data portability: Your data is stored in Apple's SwiftData format on your device. You may export it via iCloud backup.

As an Indian user, you are also protected under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act). Since we store no personal data on our servers, your rights under DPDP are primarily exercised through your device and Apple's platform.

🔒
10. Data Security

Because Swarup stores data only on your device, security depends primarily on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iPhone updated to the latest iOS version
  • Using Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode to lock your device
  • Keeping iCloud Keychain and device encryption enabled (on by default on iPhone)

Swarup does not transmit your personal preference data over the internet, which means there is no risk of a server-side data breach exposing your grooming profile.
