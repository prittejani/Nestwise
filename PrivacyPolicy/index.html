The short version

On-device AI — nothing leaves your phone

Nestwise is built on a simple promise: your parenting conversations are yours. We do not collect, store, or transmit your chat messages, your child's name, or any personal data to our servers — because we don't have any servers involved in AI processing.

All AI in Nestwise runs locally on your device using Apple Intelligence. Your data never touches the internet for AI purposes.

Information we collect

Nestwise collects the absolute minimum needed to function.

  • Your child's name and date of birth — stored locally on your device only, using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Milestone logs you mark — stored locally on your device only
  • App preferences such as theme and notification settings — stored locally via UserDefaults
  • Anonymous crash reports — only if you have opted in to Apple's crash reporting in iOS Settings

We do not collect: your name, email, phone number, location, payment details, or any information about your child beyond what you explicitly enter in the app.

How your data is used

Data stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Personalise AI responses with your child's name and age
  • Display milestone progress within the app
  • Remember your app preferences across sessions

We never use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any purpose beyond making the app work for you.

Apple Intelligence & on-device AI

Nestwise uses Apple Intelligence — Apple's on-device AI framework — to generate parenting advice. This means:

  • Your questions and conversations are processed entirely on your iPhone — they never leave your device
  • Apple Intelligence does not send your conversations to Apple's servers for AI processing
  • Nestwise cannot read or access the content of your AI conversations after they are displayed
  • Conversations are held in temporary memory and are not persisted to disk by Nestwise

Apple Intelligence operates under Apple's own privacy framework. For details on how Apple handles on-device intelligence, visit apple.com/privacy.

Third parties & data sharing

Nestwise does not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No data brokers
  • No cloud AI providers

The only third party involved in running this app is Apple — through the App Store distribution, Apple Intelligence framework, and optional iCloud backup if enabled in your iOS settings. Apple's data practices are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data retention & deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are always in full control.

  • Delete the app — all data is permanently removed from your device immediately
  • Use Settings → Reset All Data inside Nestwise to wipe all profiles and history without uninstalling
  • We cannot recover deleted data as we hold no copies

If you use iCloud Backup on your device, iOS may back up local app data as part of your standard device backup. This is controlled entirely by your iOS settings, not by Nestwise.

Children's privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

Nestwise is designed for parents and caregivers. The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (US) or under 16 (UK/EU).

Information about your child — such as their name and date of birth — is entered by you as the parent and stored only on your device. Nestwise does not transmit this information to any server.

If you believe a child has provided information to the app without parental consent, please contact us at prittejani01@gmail.com and we will assist in deleting any relevant data.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — know what data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — request removal of your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a readable format
  • Right to correct — update inaccurate information
  • Right to object — opt out of any processing

Since Nestwise stores all data locally on your device and holds no server-side copies, you can exercise all of these rights directly by deleting data within the app or uninstalling it. For any additional requests, email us at prittejani01@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

Continued use of Nestwise after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
