Enable Apple Intelligence — step by step

1

Set language to English

Settings → General → Language & Region → iPhone Language → English (United States) or English (United Kingdom)

2

Enable Apple Intelligence

Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri → Turn On Apple Intelligence

3

Wait for the model to download

Connect to Wi-Fi and keep your iPhone charging. The download takes 20–40 minutes. Track progress at Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Intelligence.

4

Confirm it's ready

You'll see "Apple Intelligence is ready" in Settings. Open NestWise — the AI chat will now work fully.

Apple Intelligence is only available on iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, and all iPhone 16 models with iOS 26.0 or later.

About NestWise

What is NestWise?

NestWise is an AI parenting guide app that uses Apple Intelligence to answer your parenting questions privately and instantly — from newborn care to toddler development.

Who is it for?

Parents and caregivers of children from newborn to school age, looking for quick, personalised, evidence-informed parenting guidance without leaving their phone.
